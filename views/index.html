Sudoku


Sudoku is a game made up of a 9 x 9 grid. The goal of the game is to fill in numbers so that you have 1-9 horizontally, vertically, and in each 3 x 3 subsection. The game ranges in difficulty and is timed. You are provided some given numbers at the beginning of the game, the goal is to fill in the remaining numbers to complete the puzzle in record time.
